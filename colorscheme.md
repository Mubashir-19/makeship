## 📱 **Usage Guidelines**

### **Background Strategy**
✨ **Keep backgrounds mostly WHITE or very light opacity washes**
- Navy: Use ONLY for text/headings
- Teal: Use extensively for highlights, accents, dividers
- Sky Teal: Very light washes (5-10% opacity)
- Avoid dark backgrounds - focus on clean white spaces

### **Navbar**
* Transparent with glass morphism blur effect
* Navy text/icons  
* Teal highlights on hover
* Orange for primary CTA

### **Brand Consistency Rule**

Use Navy + Teal as the **foundation**, and Orange **only for action triggers**.
**Maximize Teal usage throughout** - it's your differentiator.
